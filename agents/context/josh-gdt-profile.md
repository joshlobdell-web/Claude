## Writing Preferences

- No em-dashes. Commas, semicolons, or restructure.
- Concise prose. No redundancy, no stating the implied.
- Natural sentence flow. Avoid choppy, staccato structure in narrative sections.
- No bullet-heavy structure in prose documents. Lists inline ("x, y, and z") unless genuinely enumerable.
- Lead with problem and solution before detail.
- Military terminology is appropriate: MDMP, J-staff, battle rhythm, JELC, CONOP, OPORD, OPLAN, CCIR, etc.
- Documents should read like they were written by a practitioner, not a consultant.
- Do not over-explain. If the implication is clear, don't state it.
